Problematics

There are a lot of concerning and debating news and discussions about covid. Some of them are spreading fake information, some of them just sending panic messages. This work explores tweets and news realted to covid in order to find the most redundants topics appearing in the texts, to get a grasp of the mindset of the people living this pandemic : their feelings, their concerns. Also, how does the news content compare to the tweets content? Do we find the same words, the same concerns, the same raising questions when talking about covid?
