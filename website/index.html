Problematics

There are a lot of concerning and debating news and discussions about covid. Some of them are spreading fake information, some of them just sending panic messages. The vizualisations explore the statistics of the texts and find the most redundants topics or words appearing in the texts, to get a grasp of the mindset of the people living this pandemic : their feelings, their concerns. The idea is also to see how the news content compares to the tweets content, to see if the news relates closely or not to what the population is really experiencing. Are the news using the same lexical field to talk about coronavirus?
